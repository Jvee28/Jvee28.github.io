Some puppies can weight 1-1.5 kg (1-3lb), while larger ones can weight up to 7-11 kg (15-23lb). All healthy puppies grow quickly after birth. A puppy's coat color may change as the puppy grows older, as is commonly seen in breeds such as the Yorkshire terrier. Puppy refers specially to young dogs, while the pup may be used for other animal such as wolves, seals, giraffes, guinea pigs, rats or shark.
